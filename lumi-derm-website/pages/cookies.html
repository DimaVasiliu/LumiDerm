Cookie Policy

How this website stores your choice and controls optional Square and Google content.

What cookies are

Cookies are small files stored on your device by websites. They can support essential website operation, remember your preferences, measure traffic or connect third-party services.

Cookies and similar technologies we use

  • Essential choice record — this site stores consent record version 2, your selected categories and a timestamp in local storage.
  • Online booking (Square) — Square is not contacted on page load. It loads only after you allow external media or select the one-time booking load button. Square may then present its own cookie choices.
  • Maps (Google) — Google Maps is not contacted on page load. It loads only after you allow external media or select the one-time map load button.
  • Analytics — no analytics script is currently included in the website code.

Your choices

On your first visit, choose Allow external media or Essential only. You can also load either provider once without changing the saved category. Use the permanent Cookie settings button in the footer to change your saved choice later.

Managing cookies

You can also block or delete storage in your browser settings. Square booking and Google Maps remain available through clear links when optional embeds are not loaded.
