Cookie Policy

A clear starter cookie notice for essential website functions, future analytics and third-party embeds.

What cookies are

Cookies are small files stored on your device by websites. They can support essential website operation, remember your preferences, measure traffic or connect third-party services.

Cookies and similar technologies we use

  • Essential — a small record of your cookie choice so we don't ask again.
  • Online booking & payment (Square) — when you open or use the booking and payment flow, Square sets cookies needed to run it securely. These load on the booking page.
  • Maps (Google) — the embedded map in our contact section is provided by Google and may set cookies governed by Google.
  • Analytics (Cloudflare Web Analytics) — privacy-first, cookieless website measurement. It does not use cookies and does not track you across other sites.

Your choices

On your first visit, a banner lets you choose Accept all or Essential only. Essential functions and our cookieless analytics keep the site working and measured; optional cookies are used only with your consent. You can change your choice any time by clearing this site's cookies in your browser.

Managing cookies

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings. Some embedded services — the booking widget, the map or social links — may not work fully if cookies are disabled.
