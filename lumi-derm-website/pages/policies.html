Booking Policy

Appointments and consultation

Appointments are offered subject to availability, consultation outcome and treatment suitability. Some treatments may require a consultation, medical history review, patch test or cooling-off period before treatment can go ahead.

  • Clients should provide accurate contact details and relevant medical information before treatment.
  • Please arrive on time. Late arrival may reduce treatment time or require rescheduling if the appointment cannot safely be completed.
  • Prices shown online are guide prices unless confirmed during consultation or through the booking system.
  • Offers and packages may have separate expiry dates, eligibility rules or treatment intervals.
Cancellation Policy

Cancellations, deposits and no-shows

Please give as much notice as possible if you need to cancel or move an appointment. Lumi Derm Aesthetics may charge a reasonable cancellation or no-show fee where notice is short and the appointment cannot be rebooked.

  • Deposits may be requested for selected appointments, courses or longer treatment slots.
  • Any cancellation charge should reflect the clinic's direct loss, reserved time and reasonable administration costs.
  • If the clinic needs to cancel or rearrange an appointment, the client will be offered a new appointment or any appropriate refund.
  • If you are unwell, recently had sun exposure, are taking new medication, or have a skin reaction, contact the clinic before attending.
Treatment Suitability

Consent, results and safety

All treatments are subject to professional assessment. A treatment may be refused, postponed or adjusted if it is not suitable on the day or if the required safety information has not been provided.

  • Results vary by skin type, treatment plan, lifestyle, health history and aftercare.
  • No treatment result is guaranteed. Recommendations are based on the information available during consultation.
  • Some treatments may involve temporary redness, sensitivity, swelling, bruising, peeling or downtime.
  • Clients must follow preparation and aftercare guidance to reduce avoidable risk.
Aftercare

Client responsibilities

Aftercare is part of the treatment plan. Please follow the aftercare instructions provided for your treatment and contact the clinic promptly if you have concerns after an appointment.

  • Avoid using active skincare, sunbeds or strong heat where advised after treatment.
  • Use SPF and avoid unnecessary sun exposure after light, peel, microneedling and rejuvenation treatments.
  • Do not pick, scratch or exfoliate treated skin unless advised.
  • Seek urgent medical advice if you experience symptoms that feel severe or unexpected.
Complaints & Contact

How to raise a concern

If you are unhappy with any part of your appointment, contact Lumi Derm Aesthetics as soon as possible so the issue can be reviewed fairly. Please include your name, appointment date, treatment and a short description of the concern.

Complaints are reviewed using appointment notes, consultation records, photographs where relevant and communication history. The clinic may invite you for an in-person review before recommending next steps.
