Privacy Policy

How Lumi Derm Aesthetics collects, uses and protects client information for appointments, treatments, communication and safe record keeping.

Last updated

21 June 2026. Please have this policy reviewed against Lumi Derm Aesthetics' final systems and UK data-protection requirements before relying on it.

Information we collect

We may collect your name, phone number, email address, appointment history, treatment preferences, consultation notes, medical and skin information relevant to treatment suitability, before-and-after photographs where agreed, payment or deposit records, and messages sent by email, phone, Instagram, Facebook or booking platforms.

Why we use your information

  • To manage bookings, consultations and treatment plans.
  • To assess suitability, contraindications, patch-test needs and aftercare requirements.
  • To keep accurate client records and respond to concerns or complaints.
  • To send appointment information and service updates.
  • To send offers or marketing only where there is a lawful basis and you can opt out.

Health information and consent

Some consultation details may be health-related and more sensitive than ordinary contact details. Lumi Derm Aesthetics uses this information only where needed for safe treatment planning, record keeping, insurance, legal obligations or with explicit consent where required.

Photography and treatment records

Photos may be used to track progress, support clinical records or create marketing content. Marketing use should only happen with clear consent. You can withdraw marketing consent, but clinical records already needed for safety, insurance or legal reasons may still be retained.

Who we share information with

We do not sell your information. We use trusted providers who process data on our behalf, including: Square (online booking, card payments and customer records), Cloudflare (secure website hosting), Google (the optional map), and our email provider for the info@lumidermaesthetics.co.uk inbox. Where legally required, data may also be shared with insurers, regulators, legal advisers or medical professionals.

Marketing & your consent

We only send offers and updates by email or text where you have opted in, and every message includes a way to unsubscribe. You can opt out at any time without affecting your bookings or care.

Your rights

You may ask to access, correct, delete, restrict or object to certain use of your personal information. Some records may need to be retained where there is a legal, insurance, safety or accounting reason.

Contact: info@lumidermaesthetics.co.uk
